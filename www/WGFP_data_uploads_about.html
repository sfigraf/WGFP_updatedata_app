How to Use

Upload the most recent data files in .txt form from WGFP project. Click "Add to master file" to update the master excel sheet. Older .txt files won't work because they have different fields than the current fields being used. The current max upload setting is 30 mb. Files are uploaded quickly but they are automatically cleaned to a viewable, usable form which often takes some time. A 10 mb file should take about 3 mins, depending on processing power.
